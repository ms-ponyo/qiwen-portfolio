Career Tab Redesign

  • Qi Wen
  • Oct 21, 2022
  • 1 min read

Updated: Jun 26, 2023

This project aimed to provide users with career resource, contents, and services which were recommended by non-connections in a cohesive and scalable way.


By redesign, new career tab showed the resource, contents, and services which is the LI's users's high-consumption content for users directly to help them consumed more easily than before. Meanwhile, this project met the business needs, that is, we needed a function or platform to carry a lot of non-connected content.


I Initiated and leaded independently the system redesign of the whole Career Tab. Responsible for the whole process from Empathize-Define-Ideation-Prototype-Test-Implement.
